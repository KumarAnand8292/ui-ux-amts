AMTS operates 268 bus routes covering 4,182 stops across Ahmedabad, from Madhavnagar (Sanand) to Bavla.

  • Route 200: Maninagar to Maninagar (39 km, 123 stops)
  • Route SP1: Ranasan Circle to Nana Chiloda (1 km, 3 stops)
  • Route 28: Lambha Gam to Netaji Nagar (23 trips daily)
  • Route 401S: Paldi to Chandkheda Gam (16 trips daily)

Our Bus Fleet

AMTS operates 1,272 buses, including 977 red buses and 315 Janmarg BRTS buses. The fleet features eco-friendly CNG buses with modern amenities like fiber seats and updated dashboards. In 2025-26, AMTS will add 445 buses, including 100 AC CNG and 4 electric double-decker buses.
